About Us

We are a food delivery website. It was founded in Spring 2018 with the help of our mentor Rakibul Hasan. We saw that there was increasing demand of an 24/7 food delivery service within India. Our aim was to satify people who work late at night from after 8pm to 7am. During these hours, there is no delivery service in India. Our target audience was university students who pull off all nighters before exams. Then it expanded to the freelancer and outsourced programmers who work for foreign companies, whose shifts start late at night.

Our founders include All the members from our team. Our hope is to create jobs and service to everyone in India at a reasonable price.
